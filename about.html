About Komal's Suits

Since opening our doors in 1975, Komal's Custom Tailors quickly established a reputation for dressing customers in high quality, custom made attire and guaranteeing seamless customer service. Today, our family-owned and -operated business serves men and women of all sizes, suiting them in the finest fabrics from around the world.

APPOINTMENTS SERVICES

 Seamless Service.

We take pride in the details - every suit, jacket and shirt we make is created to the exact specifications and personal taste of our valued customers. We want you to look and feel your best, and will go extraordinary lengths to ensure you're satisfied with your chosen style. Whether for business or pleasure, we understand the importance of creating a wardrobe that's going to exude your personality, which is why we take the time to truly hear what our customers want. We also understand that adding new pieces to your wardrobe is an investment and will work with you to find a cost-effective solution.

We remain committed to going above and beyond to make our customers happy. If our business hours are not conducive to your schedule, our professional clothiers will come to you at your home or office. To schedule an appointment, click here or call us at (508) 872-9669.

Our Team

Lachoo Khawani has been in the business of tailoring since the year of 1972. He first opened in Bellingham, MA before moving to a location on the "Golden Mile" of Route 9 in Framingham, MA. Over the years he has built up a thriving business that provides the finest clothing to clientele consisting of lawyers, doctors, businessmen, actors, professional athletes, and more. When it comes to selecting materials, Lachoo has been known to personally go to Europe and Asia to assure the quality of the materials. He has gone from Boston, MA to Seattle, WA to bring the highest quality clothing to his customers and is always willing to go on trips such as this for the sake of the client. This dedication to the individual customer is what sets apart Lachoo from all the others.
